Bio of an Angry Asian Girl:
Sandi Gau

Joined the military at 18, graduated college at 27, became a licensed stock broker at 28 and now a wannabe developer. I work full-time as an Application Support Analyst for a software company but I continue to look for my chance to break into the development world. The brand I created for myself is the plum blossom. Plum blossom is the national flower of Taiwan and it is a flower that defies adversity and blooms in the coldest of winter. I believe you can only be the best version of yourself if you continue to accept and overcome challenges. This is very much my approach to web development and life itself.
